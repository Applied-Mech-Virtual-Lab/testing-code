Bell Crank Lever

Objective

To verify the law of moments using Bell Crank Lever.


Apparatus used:

Bell Crank Lever, Weights, Spirit level etc.


Description

Bell crank lever is used to verify the law of moments by changing the weights and their distances along the lever

STEP  1 

Make sure to tighten the string using the screw making larger arm horizontal (Use pointers and the sprit lever to check).
